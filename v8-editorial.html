We Build Things and Break Things

Software, Demos, and Experiments

Web & Circuits is a small team that builds software, interactive demos, and experiments with AI/ML. We'd rather show you than tell you.

The best experiments begin with curiosity. Not the kind that seeks answers, but the kind that revels in questions. Web & Circuits exists in this liminal space—between knowing and wondering, between building and dreaming.

We work in public because it keeps us honest. When experiments are visible, failures become lessons. When demos are interactive, ideas become tangible. This is the foundation upon which we build.

"The future belongs to those who tinker, experiment, and share what they learn."

Our projects span the spectrum from practical software to interactive demos and AI/ML experiments. We like making things you can click on, poke at, and learn from.

We don't claim to have all the answers. We don't pretend our code is perfect. What we offer instead is a commitment to exploration, a willingness to fail publicly, and an invitation for others to join the journey.
